This Privacy Policy describes how Pomodoro Time ("we," "our," or "the application") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Data

When you use our application, we automatically collect certain information anonymously:

Application Usage Data:
  • Daily, weekly, and monthly active users
  • Average session time per user
  • User retention metrics (Day 1, Day 7, Day 30, Month 2, Month 3, Month 6)
  • Number of pomodoros completed per day (aggregated from all users)
  • Hourly usage distribution based on user's local time
  • Time the application remains in the foreground
  • Application settings used (vibration mode, keep screen on)

2.2 Location Data

  • Approximate city and country (based on IP address)
  • Note: We do not collect precise GPS location

2.3 User-Provided Data

  • Application settings (work duration, breaks, preferences)
  • Language preferences
  • Dark/light mode settings

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Improve the application: Analyze usage patterns to optimize features and performance
  • Statistical analysis: Understand how users interact with different features
  • Technical support: Identify and resolve technical issues
  • Product development: Inform decisions about new features
  • Performance analysis: Monitor application performance and stability

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent: When you explicitly accept data collection through analytics settings
  • Legitimate interests: To improve and maintain application functionality
  • Compliance obligations: To comply with applicable regulations

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party services that may have access to your data:

  • Google Firebase Analytics: For usage and performance analytics
  • Google Play Services: For basic Android functionalities

5.2 We Don't Sell Personal Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

6. Your Rights and Controls

6.1 Data Collection Control

  • You can disable analytics data collection at any time through the app settings
  • Disabling does not affect the core functionality of the application

6.2 GDPR Rights (for EU users)

  • Access: Right to know what data we have about you
  • Rectification: Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Right to request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Right to receive your data in structured format
  • Objection: Right to object to certain types of processing

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data:

  • Data encryption in transit using HTTPS
  • Data anonymization whenever possible
  • Limited data access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security reviews

8. Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, you can contact us:

  • Email: pomodorotime.app@gmail.com
  • App: Through the application settings

This privacy policy complies with GDPR, CCPA, and Google Play Store policies.
